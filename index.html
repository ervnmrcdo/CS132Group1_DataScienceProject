Philippine Regional Analysis

A Data Science Approach

This project examines two key objectives to understand regional economic dynamics in the Philippines:

  • Objective 1: Geographic Center of Gravity Migration - Tracking how economic centers shift across regions over time
  • Objective 2: Investment Density by Region - Analyzing investment concentration and distribution patterns

Using data-driven insights to reveal spatial and temporal trends in Philippine regional development.

Project Overview

Understanding regional dynamics through spatial analysis

Motivation

Regional economic disparities in the Philippines have significant implications for policy-making and development planning.

Problem

Understanding how economic activity migrates and where investments concentrate is crucial for targeted development strategies.

Solution

Apply spatial analysis techniques to identify center of gravity migration patterns and investment density clusters.

Research Objectives

Two complementary analyses for regional economic insights

Objective 1

Geographic Center of Gravity Migration

This objective focuses on tracking how the geographic center of economic activity shifts across Philippine regions over time. By calculating the weighted centroid of economic metrics, we can visualize and quantify the direction and magnitude of regional economic migration patterns.

  • Temporal analysis of economic center movement
  • Regional migration patterns
  • Growth trajectory visualization

Objective 2

Investment Density by Region

This objective analyzes how investment capital is distributed across different Philippine regions. By mapping investment density, we identify concentration hotspots and underserved areas, providing insights for balanced regional development planning.

  • Investment heat mapping by region
  • Density concentration analysis
  • Regional investment disparities
